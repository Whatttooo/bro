What do we care for?

Bringing it closer to Home

Kiwi culture is important! There are so many games that take the player down a path of adventure and explore some the wonders of historical events or ancient mythology. The Maori culture is rich with with opportunity. What a better medium for our New Zealand generation to learn and discover our ancestral roots than through something that they love. Games have been a tool for fun and learning for thousands of years and it's no different today.

Keeping it Pure

New Zealanders have always seen our environment as sacred and something that we all need to protect. However, it might sometimes be hard for people to either understand the impact of their actions on the environment, or simply what they could to do to make things better. We here Fog City Games believe that we can help those that don't know by educating with our new innovations on our platform.
